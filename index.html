The Research Tax — Without Clay

Based on Dockwa's own Pre-Call Research Guide, each rep completes 4 manual steps before dialing:

1. Review marina website (aesthetics, services, booking)
2. Location + competitive check (nearby Dockwa marinas)
3. Marinas.com review + inquiry data
4. Salesforce account history + incumbent MMS

~10 min per account × 50 accounts/week = 8+ hrs/week per rep not selling.

At a $50/hr fully loaded cost: ~$20k/year per rep in lost selling time.

With Clay — What Changes

Clay runs the Pre-Call Research Guide automatically, in bulk, before the rep ever opens the account:

✅ Website quality + services auto-scraped
✅ Marinas.com inquiry count auto-populated
✅ Nearby Dockwa customers auto-identified
✅ Incumbent MMS auto-detected
✅ ICP flag auto-confirmed or overridden
✅ Owner mobile number found, not just main line

Rep opens Salesforce. Everything is already there. They dial.
